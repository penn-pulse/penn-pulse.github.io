The Problem

Cardiac arrest is a leading cause of death in hospitals, with only 25% survival to discharge and a false alarm rate in ICU monitors over 85%. Current systems fail to detect early signs even though they are visible in retrospect to cardiologists.

Can artificial intelligence help us predict and prevent cardiac arrest before it happens?

Our Vision

We aim to harness the power of generative AI and advanced data analytics to detect early warning signs of cardiac arrest, enabling timely intervention and saving lives.

Our Technology

  • 10,000+ ICU patients monitored with 3-hour ECG segments sampled at 250Hz
  • Neurosymbolic AI combining deep learning and clinical reasoning
  • Text-based explanations and symbolic summaries of ECG risk patterns
